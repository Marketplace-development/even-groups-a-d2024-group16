Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I add a recipe?

    Log in as a chef and click on "Add Recipe" in the menu.

  • How can I edit my profile?

    Go to "Edit Profile" in the menu to update your personal details.

  • How can I purchase a recipe?

    Log in as a customer, search for a recipe, and click on "Buy Now" to purchase it.
